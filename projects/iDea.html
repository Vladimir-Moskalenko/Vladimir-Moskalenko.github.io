iDea


Your notes. Your MenuBar.


iDea is a re-make of another app I made, MenuText. iDea includes much

 more features, still in a very compact app. You’re able to save 3 different

documents, as well as export them to the Desktop. And all in the MacOS 

MenuBar. That means you can access these documents from any desktop.
