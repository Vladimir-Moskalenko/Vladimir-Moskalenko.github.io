MenuWeb


The free browser for the Menu Bar.


As you probably can tell from the amount of MenuBar apps I have,

I was obsessed with Menu Bar applications. And a really important

app I wanted was a MenuBar browser. I searched far and wide across

the vast internet and found only paid apps. That’s when I decided to

use XCode to make what’s now my most favorite app! It’s a totally free

browser for the MacOS MenuBar, partially with the guts of Safari; more

precisely, it uses the latest  WebKit for webpage rendering. Try it out!
