If you have a problem, check here if there's a solution/explanation! If there's none, try contacting me.

I can't open my app, it says "MacOS can't identify malware". What should I do?

MaOS can't identify me because I don't have a paid Apple Developer Account to sign them. The apps aren't malware, you can check yourself. To bypass this error, press "Ok", open System Settings/Preferences, open "Security & Privacy". Scroll down to "Security" and press 'Open app...'. Follow the popups, pressing 'Allow', 'OK', and entering the admin password if needed.

My desktop widget glitched, it shows the wrong time. How do I resolve this?

Widgets can be checked manually every certain time interval. Updating them every minute may take up too much memory over long periods of time. To solve this, open Terminal and type "killall Dock" or "killall NotificationCenter" if the first command doesn't work. Press return, and the widgets/desktop should reload and the widgets will load the correct time.

How do I quit a menubar app?

Quitting apps that are running in the menubar and have their app icon hidden by default can be done by selecting any element in the app and pressing cmd+Q. In some apps, pressing the red close button will quit the app. On others, you can type quit into the search bar.
