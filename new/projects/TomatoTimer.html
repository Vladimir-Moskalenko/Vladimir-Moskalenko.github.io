TomatoTimer


A small timer. A big to-do.


After spending some time making a simple timer app, I decided to go further.

That’s why I made TomatoTimer. Inspired by Pomodoro timers and Forest, I wanted

to make a better desktop experience. But stopped shortly after. These days, I don’t

have much time on my projects, so I left TomatoTimer as a simple timer that adds

a tomato everythime it ends. I plan on making it a Pomodoro app, but that’ll be later.
